Our idea was to create a program that notifies a cyclist when it is time to tune up their bike was born out of problem. Previously, there wasn't a simple way to know when it was time to change a bike part. The only solution was to remember when the part was last changed and how many miles you had put on it. Together, we were able to come up with a plan to create a site that could resolve this issue by working on different aspects of the program.
